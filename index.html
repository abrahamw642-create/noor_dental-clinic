Our story

Dentistry built on trust, not pressure

Noor Dental Clinic was created with a simple belief: great dental care should feel reassuring, not intimidating. Every detail of the clinic — from the way we explain treatment to the comfort of the chair — is designed to put you at ease.

We take time to understand your goals, show you exactly what we recommend and why, and never push treatment you don't need. The result is a calmer experience and healthier, more confident smiles.

Meet Dr. Wajiha Fatima, BDS — our lead dentist, who qualified in 2017 and leads every treatment plan personally with a gentle, patient-first approach.
